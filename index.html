About

After the mysterious and sudden vanishing of young Will Byers, the people of Hawkins, Indiana begin to uncover secrets of a government lab, unsettling experiments, portals to another world, and sinister monsters.

Will's mother, Joyce, desperately tries to find him, and is convinced he's in grave danger, while police chief Jim Hopper searches for answers. Will's friends, while searching for him, discover a strange girl, who is on the run from "bad men."
